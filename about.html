About

Activepoints: The best full-featured video editor for both iPhone and Android

Activepoints gives anyone with a mobile device the ability to cut, order, transition between, and export video clips into polished, high-quality movies. Activepoints has a robust feature set wrapped in an easy-to-use interface.

Activepoints is used by social media influencers, businesses, and creative people everywhere to produce compelling video content. Activepoints's powerful tools, assets, and presets put the ability to create top-notch content in the palm of your hand.

Create
Import video, images, and audio from your device. Cut, arrange, composite, and transition between your videos and images, taking advantage of Activepoints's video layer support (Layer support is dependent on the device's hardware capabilities).

Edit
Adjust how your video appears with preset color grading, blending modes, and color correction adjustment tools. Add effects, overlay graphics, text, or draw directly on your video with handwriting layers.

Share
Then, export your project at up to 4K and 60fps! Activepoints saves video directly to your camera roll and lets you share your masterpiece directly to social media, chat, and other apps.

A subscription account to Activepoints deletes the watermark, removes ads on Android devices, unlocks all of the tool presets, and provides full access to Premium Assets on the Activepoints Asset Store.
