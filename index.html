Transform your videos with KineMaster!

Features

Awesome Assets

Download incredible effects, music, stickers, fonts and much more from the KineMaster Asset Store!

Get Projects

Download KineMaster projects directly to your device! Remix them with your own pictures and videos!

Backup & Share

Save your KineMaster projects as .kine files to keep your hard work safe or share and let others to start editing where you left off!

Blending Modes

Eight different blending options allow videos and images to be overlaid creating magical, artistic effects.

Reverse

Turn time around with the reverse tool, making focal shifts, in-camera transitions, and other video edits easy.

Chroma Key

Use chroma key to composite two (or more!) videos, including an alpha mask preview and fine-tuning of the mask edges.

High Resolution

Use your high-end camera to its full potential by editing and exporting 4K video projects at up to 60 fps.

Video Layers

Play back multiple videos: up to 9 at a time, depending on the device and video resolution.
